About Me

I'm a Business Analyst with over three years of experience in supply chain planning, procurement, and data analytics - currently pursuing my Master’s in Business Analytics at UC Riverside. My journey began as a retail business owner, where I learned to drive growth through forecasting, inventory optimization, and digital strategy. Now, I combine those real-world insights with technical skills in Python, R, Excel, and Power BI to solve complex problems at the intersection of creativity and analytics - the foundation of my brand, Art Meets Analytics.

Creativity is Intelligence having fun! - Albert Einstein

Outside of my professional work, I’m passionate about photography, digital media, and visual storytelling - creative pursuits that sharpen my eye for detail and composition. These hobbies don’t just inspire me personally; they also influence how I approach data analysis and business strategy. Whether I’m framing a shot or visualizing a dataset, I’m always focused on clarity, balance, and impact. This intersection of creativity and logic continues to shape my approach as a business analyst, helping me bring both insight and imagination to every project I take on.
